Services Offered


We offer a number of services to our customers through teaming up with many mechanics and dealerships of your choosing. The offers displayed below are not a full list, but a preview of what they offer.

  • Check Engine Light

    Check engine light service provides you the trust that we have done a 60 point service check. We check using an OBDII reader what errors your check egnine light is giving, and advise you of the problems that need to be fixed. You can be have the peace of mind that your car is being fully checked with this service.

  • Wheel Alignment

    Our partners provide world class wheel alignment, giving your vehicle the precision that it needs. Keeping your tires from wearing unusually and lasting longer. It is recommeneded that you have a wheel alignment done every 6 months, over time your alignment drifts off which could lead to unusual wearing of your tires.

  • Transmission Services

    Changing the fluid in your transmission gaurantees a longer life for your engine. Over time these fluids get depleted and need to be flushed and refilled. Come on in and we can take a look for you.

  • Oil Change

    Our partners provide a wide range of oil change services, from synthetic oil for those vehciles that have been driven for many years and non synthetic oil for the new vehicles. By changing your oil every 3,000-5,000 miles or every 6 months which ever comes first, will help keep your car engine lubricated and running smoothly as possible. Ensuring a longer life of the vehicle.
